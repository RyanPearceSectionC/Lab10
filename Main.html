Cards

Spring

Description: Spring is the best time for flowers to bloom and even better for outdoor sports activities.

Time Frame: March 19th - June 19th! (92 days!)

  • Pros
  • The temperature is perfect!
  • Time for Sports!
  • Flowers everywhere! Grow them flowers!
  • Cons
  • Bugs are EVERYWHERE!
  • Tax Day...
  • Weeds and pollen.

Summer

Description: Summer is quite loved by all. It's hot which encourages activities like swimming or even eating ice-cream.

Time Frame: June 20th to September 21st! (93 days!)

  • Pros
  • Summer Break!
  • An amazing time to go swimming or even to go to the beach.
  • Good time as any to go traveling or to go out camping.
  • Cons
  • Overheat, this is especially bad when you're home and the cooling broke on a very hot day.
  • Mosquitos appear everywhere if you decide to go camping.
  • Hot foods can be enjoyed if in a cold room, otherwise it's just going to make you overheat.

Fall/Autumn

Description: Fall/Autumn is my personal favorite season as it is only slightly chilly meaning you can enjoy the outdoors but has the perks of winter as well.

Time Frame: September 22nd - December 20th! (89 days!)

  • Pros
  • Outdoor activities such as jogging can still be accomplished despite the cold.
  • The weather is still somewhat nice and no bugs. Yay!
  • The perfect season for drinking something hot/warm outside.
  • Cons
  • Sometimes you can get careless and end up accidentally getting a cold easily. Either through the weather change, being out too late or dressing too light on the earlier parts of Fall.
  • Cold/Frozen foods are sometimes unwanted during this time, you can eat cold/frozen stuff early and mid Fall but can't late Fall.
  • Wind chills can hit at any moment late Fall which kind of suck.

Winter

Description: Winter is the coldest part of the year as the grass basically gets replaced with snow. Stay in-doors and stay warm!

Time Frame: December 21st - March 18th! (88 days!)

  • Pros
  • Snowman creation can be a lot of fun!
  • More excuses to stay at home and do nothing.
  • Hot drinks such as hot chocolate is ideal for that kind of weather.
  • Cons
  • The cold can make your fingers and hands (or other parts of your body such as your legs) jitter and shake, even start to cramp or give pain in some cases.
  • If you don't have heating then you're going to freeze in your own home. Make sure to cover with plenty of blankets.
  • Snow-Shoveling... lots... and lots... of snow-shoveling.
